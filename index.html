Welcome!

What You Aut To Know was founded with the goal of educating people,
speaking to people (via keynotes or otherwise) and helping people
to understand autism.

From people who are on the autism spectrum.

make a connection in a calm way, with each other, by communicating in a
collaborative way, in the spirit of co-operation, whereby both individuals understand,
with the correct content in context, individuals can co-exist.” ​
- (C W Larkworthy, 2021)​
